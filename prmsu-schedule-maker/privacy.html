Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2026

PRMSU Schedule Maker ("the tool") is a free, browser-based tool that turns a Certificate of Registration (COR) into a weekly class schedule image. Your privacy matters, so the tool is built to keep your data on your own device.

1. We do not collect your data

Everything happens locally in your web browser. When you upload a COR, the file is read and processed on your device. The file and the information inside it (your name, student number, subjects, schedule, and so on) are never uploaded to any server owned or operated by us. We have no servers, no database, and no account system.

2. No tracking

The tool does not use cookies, analytics, advertising, or any tracking technology to identify you.

3. Third-party libraries

To work, the page loads a few open-source libraries and fonts from public Content Delivery Networks (CDNs): Tesseract.js, PDF.js, html2canvas, and Google Fonts. Your browser requests these files directly from those providers, and such requests may include standard technical information (like your IP address and browser type) as part of any normal web request. None of your COR content is sent to them. Please refer to those providers' own privacy policies for details.

4. The image you save

When you choose "Save as JPEG", the schedule image is generated in your browser and downloaded straight to your device. We never receive a copy.

5. Your responsibility

Because processing is local, you are in control of your files. Only upload a COR that belongs to you, or that you have permission to use. Keep saved images secure if they contain personal details.

6. Not affiliated with PRMSU

This is an independent, unofficial tool. It is not operated, endorsed, or affiliated with President Ramon Magsaysay State University. The university seal is shown only to identify the school on the generated schedule.

7. Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will appear on this page with a new "last updated" date.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed to the creator, Red Pogi Lang.
